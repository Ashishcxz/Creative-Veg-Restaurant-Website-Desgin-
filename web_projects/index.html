Why you should become a vegetarian

I think everyone should become vegetarian for a range of different reasons. Around 3 billion animals are killed every single day. About 125 million animals are killed every hour. Over 30 thousand animals are killed every second. If humans don not change their ways, we are going to end up extinct like dinosaurs and no one wants that. Did you know you can save the planet by just eating less meat? And if it was that easy you would think everyone would do it. However only 8% of the world is actually vegetarian.Not only are you saving our planet, but you're also going to live a longer, happier life. In addition, youl will be saving the lives of many innocent animals. That may sound like a big call but here are the facts.
